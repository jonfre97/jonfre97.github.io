Ciao, I'm Jonas!

Welcome to my personal website (which is clearly under construction). I am an astroparticle physicist currently based at the Physics Department of the Sapienza University of Rome. My research focuses on the overlap between particle physics, cosmology and astrophysics. Currently, I am mostly working on light and ultralight (WISPy) physics beyond the Standard Model, in particular axions/ALPs and Dark Photons as part of Andrea Caputo's ERC StG grant "AstroDarkLS".
