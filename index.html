Ciao, I'm Jonas!

Welcome to my personal website (which is clearly under construction). I am an astroparticle physicist currently based at the Physics Department of the Sapienza University of Rome. My research focuses on the overlap between particle physics, cosmology and astrophysics. Currently, I am mostly working on light and ultralight (WISPy) physics beyond the Standard Model, in particular axions/ALPs and Dark Photons as part of Andrea Caputo's ERC StG grant "AstroDarkLS". Furthermore, I have a strong interest in Big Bang Nucleosynthesis, in particular when considered as a tool to constrain BSM physics. I have spent the time of my PhD in Hamburg working at DESY under supervision of Kai Schmidt-Hoberg. In 2024, I have obtained the degree from the University of Hamburg. The thesis focused on dark photons, in particular on new bounds arising from solar production of dark photons and the effects of B-L dark photon dark matter. For my Bachelor and Master degree, I studied in Aachen at the RWTH. Both theses were supervised by Felix Kahlhoefer who introduced me to both the world of academia and the fascination of the dark matter problem.
